### Design System Specifications Documented

**Color Palette:**
- Shit Yellow (#F4D03F) - Primary action/highlights
- Shit Brown (#8B4513) - Secondary/shadows
- Glitch Red (#FF0000) - Alerts/errors
- Toxic Green (#39FF14) - Success/positive
- Cyberpunk Purple (#BF00FF) - Accents
- Industrial Orange (#FF6600) - Callouts
- 4 background colors (BG Raw, Dirty, Waste, Base)

**Typography:**
- Display: Bebas Neue (bold, uppercase, headlines)
- Body: Space Mono (readable, monospace)
- Accents: Permanent Marker (handwritten, chaotic)
- 7 size levels (H1-H3, body, small, tiny)

**Components:**
1. Navigation bar (with live indicator)
2. Hero section (with SVG stink lines)
3. Ticker (horizontal scroll animation)
4. Stats grid (4-column, responsive)
5. Alert section (warning/info)
6. Drops grid (3-column masonry)
7. Flush meter (6 metrics with progress)
8. Newsletter section (dual-column form)
9. Community cards (4-column grid)
10. Footer (4-column layout)

**Animations:**
1. Glitch effect (digital corruption)
2. Blink animation (live indicator: 1.2s)
3. Shake animation (movement: 0.5s)
4. Pulse animation (opacity: 0.2s)
5. Scroll animation (ticker: 20s)
6. Various hover transforms (0.15s)

**Responsive Design:**
- Mobile breakpoint: 700px
- Layouts adapt: 4-col → 2-col, 3-col → 1-col
- Typography scales with viewport
- Navigation hidden on mobile

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

**"Ugly is beautiful. The dump is the way."**

The dshit.xyz design is intentionally brutalist and unpolished:
- Raw, unfiltered aesthetic
- Industrial chaos, maximum impact
- Glitch effects and distortions
- No pretense, no polish
- Community-first, zero corporate
